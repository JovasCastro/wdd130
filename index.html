Amecameca Mexico-Volcanoes Zone

The Volcano Zone in the State of Mexico, where Amecameca is located, is a mountainous tourist region characterized by its proximity to the Popocatépetl and Iztaccíhuatl volcanic axes. It is one of the four most important sanctuaries in central Mexico. It is a site steeped in mysticism since pre-Hispanic times.
